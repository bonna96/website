Welcome to the Department of Computer Science and Engineering (CSE). Our department provides an outstanding opportunity for students to pursue an exceptional, high-level education under highly skilled faculty members. Our vision is to provide quality education in computer science and engineering to meet the growing demands of the IT industry and contribute to the technological advancement of Bangladesh.

About Us

The Department of Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) at University of Barishal, established in 2013, is the pioneer of computer science and engineering education in Bangladesh. As the first department of its kind in the country, CSE BU has consistently set the standard for excellence in education, research, and innovation.

Since its establishment, the department has been attracting the best and brightest students of the country. The department offers Bachelor’s, Master’s degrees in Computer Science and Engineering, and regularly updates the curriculum to maintain international standards.
